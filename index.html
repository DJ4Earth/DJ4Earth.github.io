What is DJ4Earth

Welcome to the NSF CSSI "Collaborative Research - Frameworks - Convergence of Bayesian inverse methods and scientific machine learning in Earth system models through universal differentiable programming". It comprises a team from the University of Texas at Austin, MIT, the University of Chicago, the University of Colorado, Boulder, Dartmouth College, and Argonne National Lab.
